What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer science that enables machines to think, learn, and act like humans.

  • Understanding language (ChatGPT)
  • Recognizing images & speech
  • Decision making
  • Learning from experience

Main Goals of AI

  • Automation
  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Perception

AI Generated Images


Types of AI


  1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)
    • Focused on specific tasks
    • Examples: Siri, Google Assistant, ChatGPT
  2. General AI (Strong AI)
    • Human-like intelligence (not achieved)
  3. Super AI
    • Beyond human intelligence
